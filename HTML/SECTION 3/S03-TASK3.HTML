Frequently Asked Questions (FAQ)

What is your return policy?
Our return policy allows you to return products within 30 days of purchase. The item must be unused and in its original packaging to qualify for a refund.
How long does shipping take?
Shipping typically takes 5-7 business days. Delivery times may vary depending on your location and shipping method chosen.
Do you offer international shipping?
Yes, we offer international shipping to most countries. Additional shipping fees and customs duties may apply based on your location.
Can I modify or cancel my order?
Once an order is placed, it can only be modified or canceled within the first 24 hours. After that, the order will be processed and shipped as is.
How can I contact customer support?
You can contact our customer support team by emailing support@example.com or by calling our toll-free number at 1-800-123-4567.
What payment methods do you accept?
We accept major credit cards (Visa, MasterCard, American Express), PayPal, and Apple Pay.
