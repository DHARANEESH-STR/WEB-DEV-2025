Chocolate Cake Recipe

Ingredients

  • 1 and 1/2 cups of flour
  • 1 cup of sugar
  • 1/2 cup of cocoa powder
  • 1/2 teaspoon of baking powder
  • 1/2 teaspoon of baking soda
  • 1/2 teaspoon of salt
  • 2 eggs
  • 1/2 cup of milk
  • 1/2 cup of vegetable oil
  • 1 teaspoon of vanilla extract
  • 1/2 cup of boiling water

Steps

  1. Preheat the oven to 350°F (175°C).
  2. In a large mixing bowl, combine flour, sugar, cocoa powder, baking powder, baking soda, and salt.
  3. Add eggs, milk, oil, and vanilla extract to the dry ingredients. Mix until smooth.
  4. Gradually add the boiling water to the batter and mix until well combined.
  5. Pour the batter into a greased cake pan.
  6. Bake for 30-35 minutes, or until a toothpick inserted into the center comes out clean.
  7. Allow the cake to cool in the pan for 10 minutes before transferring it to a wire rack to cool completely.
  8. Frost with your favorite frosting and serve!
